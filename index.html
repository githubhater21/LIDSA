LIDSA stands for Lorem Ipsum Dolor Sit Amet club


Founded in 1204 CE, Lorem Ipsum Dolor Sit Amet club (LIDSA) aims to increase the participation of youth in the policymaking process and build their capacity to change.

We are designed to equip young citizens with a better understanding of the society they live in and the challenges that it confronts.

Our aim is to help broaden their (young citizens) perspective; think critically about their socio-political construct, tap their leadership potential and acquire various skills to create a long lasting impact.

We currently run programs all over India and UAE , in addition to undertaking projects in civic engagement and public policy.
